What Is A TAI?


Our name is a mirrored abbreviation of the words, "Invest A Tree". (Very looong wierd pause...) Wait! isn't the abbreviation for that IAT? Well, it should be, but I'm pretty sure the founder heard IAT in a movie somewhere and was afraid of a copyright lawsuit. Abbreviations aside, what is this platform doing on the internet? We can best explain IAT, wait no, TAI in three crucial parts:

Carbon Credits Trading

A clear transparent and easy to use platform

Fundraising

An easier way to raise funds for your tree planting efforts and receive information on amount raised in real time

Tree(s) Management

Readily accessible information on a variety of trees including: environmental requirements, maturity and their offsetting capabilities.

OUR TARGET GROUPS



Farmers And Tree Campaigners

Users can register their products and projects for consideration and verification to be considered in carbon trading

Researchers And Tree Enthusiasts

For a fee, they can access information about tree research and relevant materials needed.

Environmentalists

These groups can create a tree growing fundraiser where people can donate. Tai will then charge a certain percentage on the amount raised.

OUR GOALS



Tree Fundraiser Prompt

Allows campaigners to create a funding call for investment and donations in their tree planting campaigns

Tree Management Prompt

Allows access to tree information including: history, type, areas suitable for growth and amount of CO2 offset by the tree

Carbon Credits Trading Prompt

Allow corporations to get carbon credits certificates at a fee and certification of the tree growers to trade their offsets for money

WHY US?



Increased Income

Farmers get compensated for their efforts towards a clean and green planet

Simplified Tree Planting Campaigns

From funding, to access, to information, tree planting campaigns couldn't get any easier

Liberating the Carbon Trading Market in the Country

Everyone can trade, everyone can buy. It's clear and transparent
